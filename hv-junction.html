FSAE HIGH VOLTAGE JUNCTION BOX

Took project lead position for high voltage junction design and assembly, ensuring rule-compliant and efficient component packaging. Effectively integrated sub-assembly into entire Working Car Assembly.

  • Owned mechanical design and layout of HV JB including components such as the Accumulator Isolation Relays (AIRs), pre-charge circuitry, and high-current fusing.
  • Performed calculations for specifying unique busbars to facilitate high-current conductivity and thermal dissipation along the tractive system path between the inverter and accumulator.
  • Spearheaded design presentations to drive communication across subsystem stakeholders and ensure technical compliance with FSAE safety regulations.
  • Effectively interfaced HV JB into tractive assembly using a series of chassis-connected tubes and vibration-dampening mounts.
